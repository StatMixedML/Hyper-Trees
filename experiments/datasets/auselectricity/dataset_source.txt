This dataset comprises five time series, each representing the electricity demand for five Australian states.
Originally recorded at 30-minute intervals, we aggregate the data to a monthly time granularity for our analysis.
This aggregation results in some months with incomplete data (when the original 30-minute observations do not span the entire month),
which we exclude from the test set to ensure evaluation on complete months only. The dataset is borrowed from

Godahewa, R., Bergmeir, C., Webb, G. I., Hyndman, R. J., & Montero-Manso, P. (2021b). Monash
time series forecasting archive. In Neural Information Processing Systems Track on Datasets and
Benchmarks .
https://forecastingdata.org/
